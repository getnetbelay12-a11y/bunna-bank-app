# End-to-End Story

## Example: member school payment
1. Member opens app
2. Logs in with OTP/PIN
3. Clicks Pay School
4. Selects school and student
5. Confirms amount
6. Pays from account
7. Gets success message and receipt
8. Notification is sent
9. Transaction is logged
10. Banker dashboard/report updates if processed by staff

## Example: loan below 20M
1. Member applies from app
2. Notification sent to member and branch loan officer
3. Branch loan officer reviews
4. Branch approves
5. Member gets approval notification
6. Loan moves to disbursement
7. Member receives disbursement notification
8. Repayment reminders start later

## Example: loan above 20M
1. Member applies from app
2. Branch reviews
3. Branch escalates to district
4. District reviews
5. District escalates to head office
6. Head office approves/rejects
7. Member gets status notifications at every stage
8. Managers can track aging and pending approvals on web

## Example: annual shareholder vote
1. Head office creates a vote event for Board Election 2026
2. System sends voting notifications to all shareholder members
3. Shareholder logs in and opens the Voting tab
4. Member selects a candidate and confirms with OTP
5. System verifies the member is a shareholder and has not voted before
6. Vote response is stored and audit log is recorded
7. Member sees a success message and can view the vote in My Votes
8. Managers monitor participation and branch-level turnout on web
9. When voting closes, results are published to shareholders
